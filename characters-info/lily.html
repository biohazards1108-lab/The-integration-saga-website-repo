LILY

Subject ID: 02 // Outbreak Enigma

Innocent Smart Pattern Recognition Outbreak Witness

Profile dossier

Age 18. Dirty brown hair, wasteland clothing, soft posture. Lily carries no infection marks, yet her presence feels tied to the outbreak in ways she doesn’t understand. Her innocence contrasts sharply with the ruined world behind her.

She is childish at times, pure in her reactions, but frighteningly smart when it comes to patterns, symbols, and environmental clues. She notices things others miss — not because of powers, but because she pays attention.

Embedded lore

Lily’s node contains subtle metadata tied to her past and her connection to Eden. Nothing supernatural — only hints, scars, and expressions that align with the Integration Saga’s grounded realism.

  • Lily recalls a location she should not remember — tied to early Eden experiments.
  • Her notebook contains repeating symbols found in pre-outbreak research logs.
  • Lily’s observation skill is documented — she notices structural patterns in ruined buildings.
