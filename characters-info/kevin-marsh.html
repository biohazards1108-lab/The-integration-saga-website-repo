Profile Dossier

Kevin Marsh is the central survivor of the Integration Saga. Years after the fungal outbreak that collapsed civilization, he has endured the wastelands, ruins, and infected territories while carrying the grief of losing his daughter.

His encounter with Lily — a girl holding secrets tied to the outbreak — sets him on a path toward the rumored research facility known only as Eden. Their journey reveals that the infected are not merely hunting… they are watching, remembering, and searching.
