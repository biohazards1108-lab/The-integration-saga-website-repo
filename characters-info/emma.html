EMMA MARSH

Age at Death: 12 // Kevin’s Daughter

Profile dossier

Emma Marsh appears briefly in Books 1 and 2. She is remembered as Kevin’s daughter — quiet, gentle, and deeply loved. Her loss shapes Kevin’s journey and emotional state throughout the Integration Saga.

Fungal transformation

Emma’s death is tied to the mutated fungus responsible for the outbreak. She becomes the **spore core** — not by design, not by experiment, but by tragic biological circumstance. The fungus mutated after exposure to extreme radiation, creating a new infection vector that ultimately consumed her.

Terminal notes

Emma’s node contains minimal metadata out of respect for her limited appearances and her role in Kevin’s emotional arc. Additional details unlock only when accessing Kevin’s node or Eden fungal research logs.
