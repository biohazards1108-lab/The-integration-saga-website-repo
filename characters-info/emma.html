EMMA MARSH

Survivor node — restricted access

Profile

Emma Marsh appears briefly in Books 1 and 2. She is remembered as Kevin’s daughter — quiet, gentle, and deeply loved. Her loss shapes Kevin’s journey and emotional state throughout the Integration Saga.

Fungal transformation

Emma’s death is tied to the mutated fungus responsible for the outbreak. She becomes the spore core — not by design, not by experiment, but by tragic biological circumstance.
