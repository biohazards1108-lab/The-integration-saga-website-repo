ATLAS OVERVIEW

GEOSPATIAL MODULE

This module contains official maps from Book 1 (Project Eden) and Book 2 (Site Zero). All maps are **spoiler‑safe** and designed to help readers visualize the world without revealing future plot details.

PROJECT EDEN MAP

BOOK 1

Early outbreak zones, evacuation routes, and initial Project Eden deployment sites. This map highlights the regions most impacted during the first wave of the Z‑Virus.

SITE ZERO MAP

BOOK 2

The origin site of the outbreak. This map shows the surrounding region, research zones, and restricted areas without revealing narrative events.
