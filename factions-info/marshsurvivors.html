FACTION DOSSIER

SURVIVOR CELL

Overview

The Marsh Survivors are a small, tightly bonded survivor group formed during the early stages of the Z‑Virus collapse. Led by Kevin Marsh, the group prioritizes protection, resource stability, and uncovering the truth behind Project Eden and Site Zero.

Leadership

  • Kevin Marsh — Founder & Primary Decision Maker
  • Lily — Logistics & Supply Coordination
  • Rosa — Medical Support
  • Darius — Security & Perimeter Watch
  • Emma — Communications & Mapping
  • Ben — Engineering & Repairs
  • Elias — Recon & Scouting

Motives

Survival first — truth second. The Marsh Survivors seek stability, safety, and answers regarding the origins of the Z‑Virus and the actions of the White Coats. Their long‑term goal is to expose Eden’s involvement and prevent further collapse.

Resources

  • Small safehouse network
  • Scavenged weapons and tools
  • Medical supplies (limited)
  • Hand‑drawn maps of collapse zones
  • Recovered Eden documents

Operational Zones

  • Eastern Collapse Zones
  • Abandoned suburbs
  • Forest perimeters
  • Temporary safehouses

Known Members

  • Kevin Marsh
  • Lily
  • Rosa
  • Darius
  • Emma
  • Ben
  • Elias

Threat Assessment

LOW — The Marsh Survivors are not a militarized faction. Their threat level is minimal unless provoked. However, their possession of Eden documents makes them a target for the Blackout Division.
