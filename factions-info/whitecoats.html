EDEN RESEARCH DIVISION

Overview

The White Coats are the government‑funded scientific division responsible for the creation, testing, and deployment of the Z‑Virus. Operating under Project Eden, they conducted biological research with minimal ethical oversight, prioritizing results over human cost.

Leadership

  • Dr. Halden Pierce — Lead Virologist
  • Dr. Mara Voss — Behavioral Analysis Director
  • Dr. Rowan Hale — Field Operations Coordinator

Motives

Officially: “Biological advancement for national security.” Unofficially: Controlled collapse, population reset, and long‑term environmental stabilization.

Resources

  • Underground laboratories
  • Mobile research convoys
  • Black‑site testing chambers
  • Government‑issued funding and protection

Operational Zones

  • Project Eden HQ
  • Site Zero
  • Eastern Collapse Zones

Known Members

  • Dr. Halden Pierce
  • Dr. Mara Voss
  • Dr. Rowan Hale
  • Multiple unnamed lab technicians

Threat Assessment

EXTREME — The White Coats possess the knowledge, data, and infrastructure required to manipulate the Z‑Virus at scale. Their actions directly contributed to the collapse.
