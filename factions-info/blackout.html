FACTION DOSSIER

COVERT MILITARY

Overview

The Blackout Division is a covert military unit assigned to erase all traceable evidence of Project Eden, Site Zero, and the Z‑Virus program. Operating outside standard command structures, they specialize in asset recovery, witness elimination, and data destruction.

Leadership

  • Commander R. Kade — Operational Lead
  • Lt. S. Mercer — Field Coordination
  • Tech Officer “Shade” — Data & Communications

Motives

Officially: “National security and containment.” In practice: removal of any link between the government and the Z‑Virus program, including survivors, documents, and physical evidence.

Resources

  • Unmarked transport vehicles
  • Suppressed firearms and tactical gear
  • Portable data wipes and signal jammers
  • Access to restricted air support (limited)

Operational Zones

  • Site Zero perimeter
  • Project Eden facilities
  • Collapse Zones with known survivor activity

Known Activities

  • Neutralization of survivor groups with Eden documents
  • Destruction of research sites post‑breach
  • Interception of unauthorized broadcasts

Threat Assessment

EXTREME — Blackout Division is highly trained, well‑equipped, and authorized to use lethal force. They pose a direct threat to any survivor group attempting to expose Project Eden.
